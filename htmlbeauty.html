What is "STI"?

The Subaru STI (Subaru Tecnica International) is a performance-oriented version of the Subaru Impreza, a compact car. The STI models are renowned for their high-performance engines, sport-tuned suspension, and all-wheel drive systems, making them popular among car enthusiasts, especially in motorsport circles. The STI models are typically designed with rally racing in mind, as Subaru has a rich history in World Rally Championship (WRC).
